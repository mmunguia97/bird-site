Birds are cool

Birds are vertebrate animals that can fly, jump, and even swim. Birds can be found anywhere in the world at any time of day, and come in all sorts of colors and sizes.

  • Hummingbirds are tiny, fast-flying birds that rely on nectar as a food source
  • Parrots have short, curved beaks and can mimic sounds
  • Eagles are large and have hooked beaks with sharp claws

Anyone interested in birds can keep an eye out and start recognizing their local species. To learn more about birds and other animals, visit National Geographic.
